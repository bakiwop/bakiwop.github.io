#library

Two enormous HVAC units are sitting on pallets outside the library. They're being installed on the roof tomorrow. Two maintenance workers are rolling a CRAFTSMAN tool cabinet around the library, looking for a place to stash it so the tools will be near to hand.

"Why don't we just put it in one of the study rooms?" asks the first maintenance worker. "We'll ask if it's okay."

The second maintenenace worker looks at the study room and hesitates.

The first maintenance worker asks, "Afraid it'll be stolen?"

The second maintenance worker looks to the first maintenance worker and says, "I'm afraid someone will pee on it or something."
