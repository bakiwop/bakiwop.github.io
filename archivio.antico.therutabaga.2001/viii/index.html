Jet Li To Make Kung Fu Movie
In a daring move, Jet Li, star of such movies as Lethal Weapon 4 and The One, has decided to make a kung fu movie. "This is something completely new for me," Li said. "In all of my previous movies, I have practiced the art of wushu, however in my next movie, I will be using kung fu to beat the bad guys." Li said that he was a bit fearful of trying something so radically new, "This is such a stretch for me, acting-wise, but I think the audience will like it."

Man Actually Was Working Late
Jane Strong thought her husband was having an affair. "He would always be out late, coming home at all hours of the night, and sometimes the morning. He always said that he was working late, but I never believed him." The Strongs eventually went to marriage counseling where it was shown that Mr. Strong was actually working late. "I guess I was wrong, not that I would actually admit that to him."

Michael Jackson Accidentally Grabs Back-Up Singer's Crotch
Michael Jackson was filming the new video from his latest album, Invincible, when something unusual occurred during the taping. "The scene called for Michael to twirl, jump up on his toes, then grab his crotch while he threw his hat off-stage," said back-up singer Sam Thompson, "but he grabbed my crotch instead." When asked if the hat made it off-stage, Mr. Thompson replied, "Yes. Yes it did."

Chevy Trucks Are "Like A Rock"
James Mathey, Senior Editor for Car & Driver magazine said that Chevy trucks are "like a rock". "Now, when I say they are 'like a rock', I am not referring to their toughness and durability, but rather their acceleration and cornering. I mean, these trucks just suck."

Special: Microsoft/DOJ Antitrust Settlement
What exactly were the terms of the agreement between the government and Microsoft?

Microsoft agrees to:
  • "stop being a big meanie corporation" (this is said while fingers crossed)
The government agrees to:
  • pass law banning the use of free/open-source software
  • 10 year, multi-billion dollar deal that says they can only buy Microsoft products for their computers
  • 10 year, multi-billion dollar deal that says they must purchase every upgrade that Microsoft puts out
  • stop being big, evil, capitalist pig-dogs (oops, sorry, that's the Taliban's demand)
  • create weather machines to lessen the amount of rain that falls in Seattle every year
  • have president kiss Bill Gates' butt at least once a month
  • declare Bill Gates the "supreme, high, overlord, commander of the world" in 10 years
