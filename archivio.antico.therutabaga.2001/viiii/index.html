PETA To Public: Eat Turkey
The long-standing animal rights group PETA released a press statement earlier today stating that they approved of eating turkey for Thanksgiving. "Let's face it," said Alice Savoy, spokesperson for PETA, "the damn birds are annoying, smelly, and loud. Kill as many as you can and eat them this Thanksgiving season."

Turkey Says, "What The Fuck?"
When told that Thanksgiving Day was on November 22 this year, a turkey got all flustered. "What the fuck?" he said. "I always thought Thanksgiving was on the last Thursday of November, you mean to tell me it is on the fourth Thursday of November? Shit! Run for your lives my Meleagrididae Galliformes friends! Run!

Man Found Guilty Of Murder Given 3 Months Probation
A West London man was given 3 months probation after being found guilty of murdering his girlfriend. "We didn't want to punish him at all," said Thomas Holmes, a juror. "The girlfriend made him watch Pretty Woman, that piss-ass movie with Julia Roberts, 341 times. He shoulda gotten a medal or something. Poor bloke. "
