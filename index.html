Who

I am madly, deeply, head-over-heelsy in love:

Our little family lives in a small orange house with an electric lime green door on the teeter-totting edge of the North American continent on the Oregon Coast at the top of what is basically a sandbar. We are situated thirty meters horizontally and forty meters vertically from the Pacific Ocean.

I am naught but cat furniture.

I believe that people should know how very special they are.

I am a bit of an absurdist.

I love you like a nail loves a hammer.

I had a very short story of mine, Piggly Wiggly, read by Nuzzle House on their podcast. I am absurdly happy about this.

Born at a young age, I inevitably got older. These days I can often be found brushing my teeth before bed, waking up after a good nap, and respiring with abandon. I rarely use an umbrella. When I'm not sitting behind the reference desk at a public library I'm dreaming about living somewhere with beau coup snow so I can shovel lots then sit by the fire reading a book.
