Welcome

Howdy. This a place for my stuff. I'm glad you're here.

About me: I was born in the previous millennium. I am over half a century old. I live here. I am mildly alarmed to be living in a place known as the Ring of Fire, a place known for earthquakes, tsunamis, volcanoes, sneaker waves, tornadoes, landslides, wildfires, and an unfortunate amount of bird droppings. There are also large trees. I believe love is usually the answer, and have a wife (and had a cat) who show(ed) me this daily. I believe people should know how very special they are. I am a bit of an absurdist. I am a librarian.
