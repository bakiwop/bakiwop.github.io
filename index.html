Welcome

This is a place for my stuff. It's a forum for my musings. I'm glad you're here.

Groovy thing. Some of my stories have been read in Glen Reads Books (to you) by Nuzzle House. Piggly Wiggly is available here. A couple stories from The Little Oregon Book of Horrors are available here. A couple more stories from The Little Oregon Book of Horrors are available here.

About me. I was born in the previous millennium. I am over half a century old. I live here. I am mildly alarmed to be living in a place named the Ring of Fire, a place known for earthquakes, tsunamis, volcanoes, sneaker waves, tornadoes, landslides, wildfires, and unfortunate amounts of bird droppings. There are also large trees. I believe people should know how very special they are. I am a bit of an absurdist. I work in the book mines.

¡I'm unknown by billions!

About this site. It's made by hand with Notepad, html, css, and GIMP. Hosting is free via Github. I splurged for the domain name. There's no advertising. No affiliate links. Privacy policy? Data is neither collected nor stored. Embedded media is self-hosted (sometimes to your detriment...things can run slowly). Other media is linked without tracking on my part. Author links go to their website or publisher's page. Book links go to WorldCat, a place which shows you the libraries nearest you that have that book.

Contact me. I am not on social media. If you would like to get in touch, text me at 541-351-8354‬.
